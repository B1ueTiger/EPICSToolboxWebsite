Carbon Fiber

Carbon fiber is a unique composite material used in racing history to make the body of racing cars. It is very stiff and lightweight, making it ideal for racing. It is made with lots of fibers bound together in layers and complex figures, as shown in the diagram to the left.



This pace car to the right utilizes a carbon fiber body, whose design was influenced by a team of engineers with materials science and physics knowledge.
