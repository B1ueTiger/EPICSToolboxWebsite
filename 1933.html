1933:

1933: In 1933, a record of 42 cars started the 500. For 1934, a maximum fuel consumption limit was imposed, 45 US gal (37 imp gal; 170 l). It became 42.5 US gal (35.4 imp gal; 161 l) in 1935 and 37.5 US gal (31.2 imp gal; 142 l) in 1936. The limits were abandoned after several competitors ran out of fuel in the closing stages, though the use of pump gasoline was still mandatory.
