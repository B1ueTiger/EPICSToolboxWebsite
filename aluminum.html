Aluminum

Aluminum is a strong, flexible, and lightweight metal used within all Indy racing vehicles! Because aluminum is so lightweight and long-lasting, it is preferred over steel.

You can find many aluminum parts within racing vehicles! Parts such as the bird cage, the enginer, and the wheels are made up of aluminum alloys. These aluminum alloys are used a lot in motor sports because they are very strong and can be easily machined.
